About

From a young age, I've been captivated by the dynamic world of technology, from gaming consoles to cutting-edge smartphones. This passion seamlessly integrates into my daily life, propelling me to explore and excel in the realm of computer programming. My educational journey began at Open Meadow High School in Portland, Oregon. Despite facing challenges that led to a temporary departure, I refused to let setbacks define me. In 2020, I obtained my GED, showcasing resilience and commitment.


In 2021, I embraced a new chapter at The Tech Academy, grateful for the opportunities it offered. Setting my sights on greater goals, I dedicate myself wholeheartedly, driven by a belief in continuous improvement and an insatiable thirst for knowledge. Technology is not just an interest; it's an unwavering passion. Through my journey at The Tech Academy, my resilience in overcoming challenges, and my commitment to growth, I'm poised to make a lasting impact in the ever-evolving world of technology.
