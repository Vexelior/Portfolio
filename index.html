About

Ever since I was a child, the captivating world of technology has held me spellbound. From the latest gaming consoles to cutting-edge smartphones, I have always been enthralled by the advancements in the tech industry. This passion for technology has seamlessly integrated into every aspect of my daily life, driving me to explore it further. To pursue my dreams and immerse myself in the world of computer programming and technology, I have deciated myself to be a passionate software developer, continuously learning and imrpoving my skills.

My educational journey began at Open Meadow High School in the vibrant city of Portland, Oregon, my beloved hometown. Unfortunately, due to personal circumstances, I had to make the difficult decision to drop out. However, I refused to let this setback define me. Determined to continue my education, I obtained my GED in 2020, proving my commitment and resilience.

In 2021, I embarked on a new chapter by joining The Tech Academy. I am immensely grateful for the opportunities and accomplishments that have come my way. As I set my sights on achieving even greater goals, I wholeheartedly dedicate myself to my work, pouring my passion and drive into every project. I firmly believe that there is always room for improvement and endless knowledge to be gained, and I am committed to continually expanding my skill set.

Technology has always been more than just an interest to me; it is my unwavering passion. Through my educational journey at The Tech Academy, my tenacity in the face of challenges, and my commitment to growth and collaboration, I am poised to make a lasting impact in the ever-evolving world of computer programming and technology.
